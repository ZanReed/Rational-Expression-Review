Using the graphs below, notice how $f(x)=\log x$ and $f(x)=10^x$ are reflections of each other across the line $y=x$.

$f(x) = \log x$

Key insight: $\log_b a = x \iff b^x = a$. The log and exponential are inverses — their graphs are mirror images across $y = x$.
